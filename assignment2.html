Assignment 2 : Face Generator


For this assignment, I created a simple generator that produces multiple variations of a form by exploring its parameters. Using code, I designed a system that allows for dynamic adjustments through mouse and keyboard interactions, generating unique iterations of the same base design. This project highlights the power of parameterized design, where I acted as a conductor orchestrating a range of outcomes rather than crafting a single static piece. The result is a versatile and interactive exploration of design possibilities.
