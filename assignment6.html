Assignment 6 : Data Portrait


This project involved creating a data-driven self-portrait based on a personal habit I tracked over a week. By collecting 20-50 data points and translating them into an array in p5.js, I visualized the habit through an animated portrait. The animation reflects patterns in my behavior, using programming concepts learned throughout the course. The final output includes an instructional guide to help viewers interpret the visualization, blending personal reflection with creative data representation.


DATA PORTRAIT DESCRIPTION :

This year, I set a goal to cultivate the habit of reading, listening to podcasts, or journaling each day. This idea comes from my desire to portray my personal habits of reading and watching podcasts using p5.js. Through reading and listening to podcasts, I often find new quotes that resonate with me. In this sketch, you can see a series of my quotes of the day from Friday, October 11, 2024, to Thursday, October 17, 2024.
